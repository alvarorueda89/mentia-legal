Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Mentia ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered study companion for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifiers provided by those services.

Study content: Materials you upload or create (text, documents, photos, audio recordings) are stored in our cloud infrastructure to provide the app's study features.

Usage data: We collect anonymized usage analytics such as quiz scores, study session activity, and feature usage to improve the app experience.

Subscription data: Purchase and subscription status is managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the app's study features (summaries, flashcards, quizzes, audio lessons, podcasts)
  • To process your content using AI services (OpenAI, ElevenLabs) for generating study materials
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve the app and fix issues

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, data storage, cloud functions, and file storage
  • OpenAI — AI content generation (summaries, quizzes, flashcards, audio)
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice generation for podcasts
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management

Your study content is sent to AI providers for processing. We do not sell your data to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase infrastructure with industry-standard security measures. Data is transmitted using encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the app's Settings screen. Account deletion permanently removes all your data, including:

  • All study materials and generated content
  • Quiz results and study progress
  • Audio files and recordings
  • Your user profile and authentication data

6. Children's Privacy

Mentia is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your study materials
  • Opt out of non-essential data processing

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at mentiacontact@gmail.com.
